Advocate!
2023
This project is about raising awareness on a social issue through designing a series of campaigns. I've chosen to advocate for animal rights, specifically focusing on the topic of dog ear cropping. I've noticed that contemporary society's pursuit of personal aesthetic perfection has extended to pets, causing anxiety over their appearance as well.
Many businesses, in an effort to make dogs look more handsome, will crop the ears of breeds like Dobermans into sharp triangles, or round the ears of other breeds to make them appear cuter. This practice is extremely cruel and unethical. It is an unhealthy and painful process for dogs. Through my series of designs, I hope to give a voice to dogs and draw public attention to this issue.
