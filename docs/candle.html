APINK Candle
2020
This is a branding project. I designed a brand of scented candles. The difference between my candles and other brands is that the shapes of the candles I made are the shapes of various small cakes. The colors are very rich and look very appetizing. At the same time, the smell is sweet like a cake. This is a scented candle that is very suitable for young girls around the age of 20. Therefore, the logo and product design will be more cute and closer to the girl’s feeling. I hope everyone’s life after using this candle can be As sweet as a cake.
