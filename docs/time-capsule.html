Time Capsule
2024
In the electronic era we live at, even the method of payment has transformed from physical cash transactions to mobile payments, where everyone merely needs a phone when stepping out. Wallets and paper money have disappeared in this era, gradually becoming fossil-like relics. The memories once specifically associated with wallets have also vanished; a wallet used to carry not just money, but also many traces of life, such as important photos and ticket stubs. Now, all memories are digital, and people can no longer touch these paper mementos.
