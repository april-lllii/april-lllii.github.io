Place & Space
2023
The main color I use for the cover of the entire book is yellow, because this color ’s pillow used to be the longest pillow I had in my childhood. The yellow florals on the first layer are pillow, and the yellow wool cloth on the second layer serves as a layered foil.
The content in the book is designed by some dream scenes associated with my dreams. The first page is a childhood dream of playing with friends, so the colors and decorations are very bright. The second page is a dream about love, so the theme is the three-dimensional woolen thread, and the scene told is the process of falling in love. The overall style of the third page is different from the previous . It was a nightmare. I fell into the whale pond in the aquarium and couldn’t escape for a long time, so I used a lot of messy line segments to express my inner mood at that time.
